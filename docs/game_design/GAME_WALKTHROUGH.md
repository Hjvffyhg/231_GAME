# Space Survival: Sector Alpha Walkthrough

**Recommended Level:** Level 1 (New Deployment)
**Estimated Time:** 15 - 20 Minutes
**Quest Giver / Start Location:** Prototype Hangar (Earth Defense Initiative)
**Prerequisites:** OS Boot Sequence Completed

## 🎒 Preparation & Loadout

Before starting this section, make sure you have the following equipped or in your inventory:

- **Consumables:** None initially. Health (Green) and Ammunition (Amber) crystals drop dynamically during combat.
- **Weapons/Gear:** Base Pulse Cannon (Primary Fire), Shield Module (Passive).
- **Skills/Magic:** Default OS Drone (Level 1 CPU Core).
- **Warning:** The system begins on **FCFS (First-Come, First-Served)**. Your drone will lock onto enemies strictly in the order they spawn. If a heavy unit is targeted, the drone will ignore all other threats until it is destroyed.

## 🗺️ Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Part 1: The FCFS Protocol (Waves 1-3)

1. **Initial Deployment:** As soon as you spawn in the void, use WASD to start moving immediately. Monitor the Tactical Map (top right corner) to track incoming radar blips.
2. **First Contact:** The swarm initially consists of weak Kla'ed Grunts. Your drone will automatically vaporize them in spawn order.
3. **Manual Overrides:** Never rely entirely on the drone's FCFS lock-on. Use your mouse to aim and manual fire (Left Click) to clean up stragglers that spawn late but move faster.

🏆 **SECRET / COLLECTIBLE:** Sometimes, neutral crystalline asteroids float through the combat zone. Destroying them grants bonus CTR (Credits) to spend in the Prototype Hangar!

### Part 2: The Convoy Trap (Waves 4-5)

1. **Threat Identification:** Heavy Kla'ed Tanks will begin spawning alongside fast kamikaze fighters.
2. **The FCFS Roadblock:** If a Tank spawns first, your drone will waste all DPS on its massive shield, completely ignoring the kamikazes flying directly at you.
   **Combat Solution:**

- Immediately stop shooting the tank.
- Use your Dash maneuver (Spacebar / Shift) to slip past the kamikaze charge.
- Manually aim your main pulse cannons at the fast fighters to destroy them while your drone is occupied with the tank.

### Part 3: Algorithmic Shift

1. **OS Upgrade:** Once the tanks are cleared, your system will temporarily pause and offer a kernel upgrade.
2. **Selection:** Choose the **Round-Robin (RR)** scheduling algorithm.
3. **Execution:** With RR active, your drone will now rapidly cycle its targeting laser across every enemy on screen. This will suppress large swarms evenly. Stay in the center of the arena and spin to fire at weakened enemies.

## ⚔️ Boss Fight: The Dreadnought (FCFS Executioner)

**Weakness:** Precise manual fire, agility.
**Resistances:** Drone focus fire (Massive HP pool makes FCFS targeting inefficient).

### Phase 1 [100% - 50% Health]:

- **Mechanic/Attack 1:** _Linear Hellfire_. The boss fires dense, straight lines of plasma directly at your last known position.
- **Counter Strategy:** Never reverse direction. Maintain a wide, circular strafing path around the dreadnought to spread the plasma lines thinly.
- **Mechanic/Attack 2:** _Minion Swarm_. Spawns 6 fast fighters to flank you.
- **Counter Strategy:** Let your Round-Robin upgraded drone "juggle" the minions to stall their advance while you dedicate 100% of your manual fire into the boss's core.

### Phase 2 [50% - 0% Health]:

- **New Mechanic:** _Overdrive Core_. The Dreadnought turns red, increases its movement speed by 40%, and fires predictive spread shots that aim where you are _going_, not where you are.
- **Counter Strategy:** You must "juke" the targeting computer. Move left to bait the spread, then immediately Dash right through the gaps in the bullets. Fire constantly.

## 🎁 Rewards & Loot

Upon completing this mission/boss, you will receive:

- **Key Item:** Round-Robin (RR) Algorithm Core (Permanent Unlock)
- **Weapon / Armor Piece Name:** Access to Plasma Spread Blueprint
- **Currency Amount:** ~15,000 CTR
- **EXP Amount:** 500 OS Data Points

# Space Survival: Sector Beta Walkthrough

**Recommended Level:** Level 5 (Advanced Deployment)
**Estimated Time:** 25 - 30 Minutes
**Quest Giver / Start Location:** Tactical Starchart
**Prerequisites:** Round-Robin (RR) Core Unlocked, Sector Alpha Cleared

## 🎒 Preparation & Loadout

Before starting this section, make sure you have the following equipped or in your inventory:

- **Consumables:** 2x Shield Boosters, 1x Emergency Repair Kit.
- **Weapons/Gear:** Plasma Spread (Primary Fire), Heavy Deflector Shield.
- **Skills/Magic:** OS Drone equipped with RR (Round-Robin) algorithm.
- **Warning:** The Kla'ed armada in this sector deploys Heavy Cruisers with massive service times. Relying solely on FCFS will lead to CPU starvation and death.

## 🗺️ Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Part 1: The Swarm Engine (Waves 6-8)

1. **Sustained Pressure:** Enemies will spawn in wide formations. Switch your drone to **Round-Robin (RR)** immediately to apply equal damage across the entire frontline.
2. **Ammunition Conservation:** Do not hold left-click blindly. Let the RR drone chip away at their shields, and use your Plasma Spread to finish them off in bursts.
3. **Navigating the Asteroid Belt:** Debris will block your shots. Lure the fast fighters through the dense asteroid fields so they crash and drop free CTR.

🏆 **SECRET / COLLECTIBLE:** Tucked behind a giant indestructible comet in the rightmost edge of the safe zone is a hidden cache containing an early **Dash Cooldown Blueprint** upgrade. Use your dash to slip past the comet before it leaves the screen!

### Part 2: The Logic Block (Waves 9-10)

1. **Threat Identification:** The armada stops sending weak fighters and deploys three massive Juggernauts at once, flanked by a trickle of sniper units.
2. **The Starvation Problem:** RR will spread damage too thin, and the snipers will destroy you while the drone tickles the Juggernauts.
   **Combat Solution:**

- **OS Upgrade:** The system unlocks the **Highest-Response-Ratio Next (HRRN)** algorithm prompt mid-wave. Enable it immediately.
- Your drone will now calculate `(Wait Time + Threat Level) / Threat Level`. It will instantly snipe out the high-threat snipers lurking in the back, then focus fire the Juggernauts.

## ⚔️ Boss Fight: The Kla'ed Overlord (HRRN Vanguard)

**Weakness:** Mobility, Rapid Fire, HRRN Drone utilization.
**Resistances:** Slow heavy cannons, FCFS targeting.

### Phase 1 [100% - 50% Health]:

- **Mechanic/Attack 1:** _Time-Slice Lasers_. The Overlord fires a sweeping laser that stops and starts at regular intervals, mimicking an RR slice.
- **Counter Strategy:** Time your Dash to match the gaps in the laser's cycle. Do not dash early, or you will land directly in the beam.
- **Mechanic/Attack 2:** _Priority Missiles_. Spawns slow-moving, high-damage homing missiles.
- **Counter Strategy:** Your HRRN drone will naturally prioritize these missiles once their "wait time" (time alive) makes their Response Ratio fatal. Just kite them into the drone's line of sight.

### Phase 2 [50% - 0% Health]:

- **New Mechanic:** _CPU Starvation_. The boss floods the zone with hundreds of invincible decoy ships, trying to crash your drone's targeting logic.
- **Counter Strategy:** Manually switch your drone back to **FCFS** briefly to force it to lock onto the overarching Boss (who spawned first), overriding the decoys. Pour everything you have into the exposed Overlord core.

## 🎁 Rewards & Loot

Upon completing this mission/boss, you will receive:

- **Key Item:** Highest-Response-Ratio Next (HRRN) Master Core
- **Weapon / Armor Piece Name:** EMP Blast Module
- **Currency Amount:** 30,000 CTR
- **EXP Amount:** 1,200 OS Data Points

# Space Survival: Sector Gamma Walkthrough

**Recommended Level:** Level 10 (Final Deployment)
**Estimated Time:** 35 - 45 Minutes
**Quest Giver / Start Location:** Tactical Starchart (Sector Gamma)
**Prerequisites:** All OS Cores Unlocked, Sector Beta Cleared

## 🎒 Preparation & Loadout

Before starting this section, make sure you have the following equipped or in your inventory:

- **Consumables:** 3x Health Injectors, 2x Drone Overcharge Batteries.
- **Weapons/Gear:** Photon Swarm (Primary Fire), Reactor Shielding (Level Max).
- **Skills/Magic:** Multi-Level Queue System (Ability to hot-swap between FCFS, RR, and HRRN).
- **Warning:** The Kla'ed Sovereign will dynamically counter your drone's active algorithm. You must constantly hot-swap modes to maintain DPS and avoid logic lockouts.

## 🗺️ Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Part 1: The Multi-Level Swarm (Waves 11-13)

1. **Dynamic Formations:** The armada now deploys mixed waves combining Grunts, Kamikazes, Tanks, and Snipers simultaneously.
2. **Algorithm Hot-Swapping:**
   - Use **HRRN** at the start of the wave to pick off dangerous backline Snipe processes.
   - As the Kamikaze Grunts close in, instantly switch to **Round-Robin (RR)** to juggle their advance and prevent them from detonating on your hull.
3. **Environmental Hazards:** Periodic EMP storms will sweep across the map. Staying inside the EMP zone will disable your drone entirely for 5 seconds. Use your Dash to outrun the expanding EMP shockwaves.

🏆 **SECRET / COLLECTIBLE:** During an EMP storm in Wave 12, an invisible elite interceptor will spawn. If you manage to destroy it using manual fire without your drone, it drops the final hidden **Aegis Hull** upgrade blueprint.

### Part 2: The Core Breach (Wave 14)

1. **The Funnel:** The safe zone shrinks dramatically, creating a claustrophobic tunnel littered with hardened asteroids.
2. **The Convoy Wall:** Behemoth-class processes will block the path forward.
   **Combat Solution:**

- Set your drone to **FCFS (First-Come, First-Served)**. Since the Behemoths spawn first in this section, FCFS guarantees your drone focuses all its computing power on breaking their massive shields.
- Once a Behemoth's shield drops, use your EMP Blast Module to stun it, then rush past it to the boss arena.

## ⚔️ Boss Fight: The Kla'ed Sovereign (OS Corruptor)

**Weakness:** Unpredictable player patterns, precision Photon Swarm attacks.
**Resistances:** Static algorithm reliance.

### Phase 1 [100% - 50% Health]:

- **Mechanic/Attack 1:** _Logic Scramble_. The Sovereign hacks your OS, forcing your drone into a specific algorithm (e.g., locking it to RR) while spawning enemies that counter it.
- **Counter Strategy:** You cannot rely on your drone during a Scramble. You must manually cover its weaknesses. If locked to RR, use manual fire to focus down heavy targets. If locked to FCFS, manually clear the swarm.
- **Mechanic/Attack 2:** _Process Termination_. A massive, screen-wide execution grid appears. Safe zones are marked in cyan.
- **Counter Strategy:** Dash into the cyan zones before the grid flashes magenta. Save your stamina specifically for these moments.

### Phase 2 [50% - 0% Health]:

- **New Mechanic:** _Total System Failure_. The Sovereign permanently disables your OS Drone. You are left entirely on manual control.
- **Counter Strategy:** This is a pure bullet-hell survival test. Rely entirely on your Photon Swarm and Dash i-frames. Focus 100% on dodging and fire continuously in the Sovereign's general direction. Do not get greedy—survival is priority over damage output.

## 🎁 Rewards & Loot

Upon completing this mission/boss, you will receive:

- **Key Item:** The Space Survival Kernel (Game Cleared)
- **Weapon / Armor Piece Name:** Golden Sovereign Plating (Cosmetic)
- **Currency Amount:** 100,000 CTR
- **EXP Amount:** MAX LEVEL REACHED
